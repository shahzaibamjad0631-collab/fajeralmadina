About Us

A Dubai-based team built around one workshop.

Fajer Al Madina Advertising LLC is a Dubai-based signage, printing and interior fit-out company operating out of Frij Al Murar, Deira. We work with shops, offices and growing businesses across the UAE that need branding done properly — from the first concept sketch to the final installed sign.

Because design, printing and fit-out all happen under the same roof, there's no handing your job between separate vendors. One team scopes the work, one team produces it, and one team installs it — which means fewer delays and one point of contact throughout.
